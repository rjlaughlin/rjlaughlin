About Me

My name is Riley Laughlin, and I have over nine years of experience as a mechanical design engineer.

From a young age, I’ve had a passion for sports and sports statistics, which sparked my interest in analytics. While pursuing my MBA at W. P. Carey, I developed a deeper enthusiasm for data analytics, business strategy, and data-driven decision-making. This inspired me to transition into a data-focused role where I can leverage my analytical skills to drive meaningful insights and strategic decisions. I am eager to make a different kind of impact in my workplace—one that aligns with my passion for problem-solving and long-term career aspirations.

Outside of work, I enjoy working out, golfing, and spending time with family.
